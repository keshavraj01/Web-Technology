    Ingredient

  • 6 potato
  • 1 numbers green chilli
  • 10 coriander leaves
  • water as required
  • red chilli powder as required
  • 4 tablespoon ghee
  • 1 numbers onion
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger
  • 1/2 tablespoon carom seeds
  • salt as required
  • 2 cup wheat flour

    • Direction

  • To prepare this easy recipe, wash the potatoes and put them in a pressure cooker along with water in it. Pressure cook on 2-3 whistles. Once boiled, turn off the burner and peel the potatoes in a bowl..
  • Mash the potatoes well and and red chilli powder, salt, chopped green chillies, grated ginger, chopped coriander leaves and carom seeds. Mix well all the ingredients and keep aside
  • Add wheat flour in a dough kneading plate along with a little salt. Mix well and then add water in it and knead a soft and smooth dough..
  • When the dough is ready, take out a small portion and using some dry flour roll into a chapati. Stuff this chapati with the potato filling and roll back to a ball. Using a little dry flour, roll into a chapati again..
  • Now, put a tawa on medium flame and put this roti on it. Using a little ghee, cook the paratha until a little brown on both sides.
  • Serve with butter and curd
